Why Repair Type Matters

A scratched bumper, peeling clear coat, and collision-damaged panel are not the same repair.

Some damage may be cosmetic. Some damage may require paint refinishing. Some collision damage may need deeper inspection before a proper repair direction can be given.

AutoBody San Diego helps separate bumper repair, paint repair, collision repair, and insurance-related repair requests so drivers can start in the right place.

  • Cosmetic bumper damage may not require full replacement.
  • Paint damage may need localized refinishing or a larger repaint plan.
  • Collision damage may involve hidden damage or panel alignment issues.
  • Insurance-related repairs may require documentation, estimates, or additional review.

Common Questions

Auto Body Repair Questions

A few of the questions San Diego drivers ask before sending photos.

Can I get an auto body quote from photos?
Photos can help start the repair direction, especially for bumper, paint, and visible exterior damage. Some collision or insurance-related damage may still need an in-person inspection before a final repair plan can be made.
What if I do not know whether I need bumper repair, paint repair, or collision repair?
That is exactly why AutoBody San Diego is built around damage type. Start with "not sure" and send photos so the request can be reviewed from the right direction.
Do I need insurance to request help?
No. AutoBody San Diego can help organize both customer-pay and insurance-related repair requests.
What photos should I send?
Send a close-up of the damage, a wider photo showing the damaged area on the vehicle, and any additional angles that show cracks, dents, scratches, paint damage, or panel alignment issues.
What areas do you serve?
