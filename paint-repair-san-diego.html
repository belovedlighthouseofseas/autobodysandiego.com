Why a Paint Specialist

Paint is its own craft — not an afterthought to body work.

A lot of San Diego "auto body" shops will quote you on paint as if it's a side service. Paint specialists do nothing else. That means controlled spray environments, proper primer prep, and color matching that's been done thousands of times on the same paint codes.

The difference shows up in two places: how the panel looks under sunlight, and how the paint holds up two years from now. Specialist work blends seamlessly and lasts.

If the panel itself is damaged, we'll route you to collision repair first. Body work first, paint last — never the other way around.
