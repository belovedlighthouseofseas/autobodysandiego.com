Why a Collision Specialist

Collision repair is structural — not just cosmetic.

The thing most San Diego drivers don't know: a car that looks "fixed" after a collision can still have hidden frame damage that affects safety, resale value, and how your car handles in the next accident.

Certified collision shops measure the frame, replace structural parts to factory specs, and recalibrate safety systems. That's the difference between a real repair and a cosmetic patch.
