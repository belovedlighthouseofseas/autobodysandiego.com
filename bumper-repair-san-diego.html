Why a Bumper Specialist

A full body shop is overkill if the rest of the car is fine.

Most San Diego drivers default to "body shop" the second something happens. But if the only damage is on the bumper cover, that's a different kind of work — and a different price.

Bumper repair specialists handle plastic repair, sanding, primer, color-matched paint, and clear coat as a focused workflow. They're not pulling frame measurements or aligning panels. That means faster turnaround, lower cost, and a finish that blends into the rest of the car.

If the damage is bigger than the bumper, that's a different path — we'll route you to collision repair instead.
