# SESSION 4 — AutoBody San Diego Homepage Full Buildout

## The Main Traffic Headquarters Page

> Status: Locked, v1.0. Canonical reference for the homepage layout, copy, section order, and SEO/schema structure. Read before editing the homepage or making cross-page changes that affect homepage anchors.

The homepage has one job:

**Turn broad San Diego auto body traffic into organized repair leads.**

It cannot be built like a regular shop homepage. A regular shop says "We do collision repair / paint / insurance / contact us today." That is too basic. AutoBody San Diego opens with a stronger structure:

> What happened to your vehicle? Choose the damage type. Send photos. Get repair direction.

That is the whole conversion engine.

---

## 1. Homepage Objective (5 things it must do)

1. Make it instantly clear this is about auto body repair in San Diego.
2. Let the visitor choose their damage type.
3. Route visitors into bumper, paint, collision, accident, or insurance pages.
4. Push photo-based quote requests.
5. Build local SEO authority for San Diego and nearby areas.

Within 5 seconds the visitor should understand: *"This site helps me figure out what repair path I need."*

---

## 2. Homepage Primary Message

**San Diego auto body help, matched to the right repair path.**

Better than "Best auto body repair in San Diego." Customers aren't ready to be sold — they're trying to understand what happened, what it costs, and what kind of repair they need. The homepage is built around **guidance first, conversion second** — but the guidance always leads toward the quote funnel.

## 12. FAQ Preview (§10)

5 questions in collapsible `<details>` cards:

1. **Can I get an auto body quote from photos?**
   Photos can help start the repair direction, especially for bumper, paint, and visible exterior damage. Some collision or insurance-related damage may still need an in-person inspection before a final repair plan can be made.

2. **What if I do not know whether I need bumper repair, paint repair, or collision repair?**
   That is exactly why AutoBody San Diego is built around damage type. Start with "not sure" and send photos so the request can be reviewed from the right direction.

3. **Do I need insurance to request help?**
   No. AutoBody San Diego can help organize both customer-pay and insurance-related repair requests.

4. **What photos should I send?**
   Send a close-up of the damage, a wider photo showing the damaged area on the vehicle, and any additional angles that show cracks, dents, scratches, paint damage, or panel alignment issues.

5. **What areas do you serve?**
   AutoBody San Diego focuses on San Diego and surrounding areas including Lemon Grove, Spring Valley, La Mesa, El Cajon, National City, Chula Vista, Mira Mesa, Kearny Mesa, Santee, Poway, and nearby areas.
